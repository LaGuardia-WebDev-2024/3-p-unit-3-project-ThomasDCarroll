My Rating: ✦✦✦✦✦

Little Hope

My Rating: ✦✦✦✦

  • I love me a Supermassive game (Until Dawn, anyone?).
  • Taylor was MISUNDERSTOOD. Im convinced anyone would act the way she did if faced with their situation
  • Me and my brother played the couch co-op with my mom, and we somehow got everyone out alive, so I dont know if I would say its a hard game...

Little Nightmares

My Rating: ✦✦✦

  • After hearing so many good things about Little Nightmares, and remembering it being really good, I was so excited to finally play it myself. Unfortunately, I dont know if it lived up to my expectations.
  • Dont get me wrong, Little Nightmares was good, but its length, and specifically the lack of difficulty made it so the game didnt have that much of an impact on me.
  • I will say, however, that the Geisha was an amazing final boss, and I really enjoyed the ending!
